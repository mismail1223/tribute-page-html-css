Biography

General Pervez Musharraf NI(M) HI(M) TBt (Urdu: پرویز مشرف, romanized: Parvez Muśharraf; 11 August 1943 - 5 February 2023) was a Pakistani military officer and politician who became the tenth president of Pakistan after the successful military takeover of the federal government in 1999. He also served as the 10th Chairman Joint Chiefs of Staff Committee from 1998 to 2001 and the 7th Chief of Army Staff from 1998 to 2007.
